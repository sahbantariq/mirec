About MIREC

Management Institute for Real Estate and Construction

Institute

MIREC provides professional training and certifications to brokers, contractors and real estate agencies in the field of real estate management. MIREC was founded in November, 2018 by Sahban Tariq Malik with an aim to bring a revolution in the real estate industry. The training and certification programs offered by MIREC will equip its members with the latest management tools in the real estate sector and enable them to be successful and reliable realtors.

Our Mission

To be a leading institute that provides management training and certifications to real estate brokers and developers.

Our Vision

To form a research based university that provides various specializations in real estate and construction industry.

Founder & Philosophy

Sahban Tariq Malik

Sahban Tariq Malik is a budding entrepreneur in the field of real estate and construction. Having obtained his bachelors degree in Accounting and Finance from Lahore University of Management Sciences - LUMS and masters in Quantitative Methods from Marmara University, Istanbul, he co-founded a real estate marketing agency in July 2017, named Beacon Investment. Within the first year, he brought a huge success to Beacon Investment through an effective business strategy, his persuasive management style and inspiring personality.

Mr. Sahban strongly believes that introducing an effective business strategy will make real estate brokers and brokerage firms more efficient and reliable than the conventional real estate agency framework. This will not only give the brokers a comparative advantage, but it will also benefit investors and end consumers as it will increase the level of trust and reliability in the sector. Therefore, Mr. Sahban founded this organization with a mission to bring a revolution in the industry by providing training and certification to those who aspire to lead.

Another project by Sahban Tariq Malik

Beacon Investment

Co-founded by Mr. Sahban Tariq Malik, Beacon Investment is a full service real estate company, located in Islamabad, Pakistan, that provides acquisition, development and finance expertise for both commercial and multi-family projects.

The mission of Beacon Investment is to provide its investors with the most honest, reliable and efficient real estate services in Pakistan.
